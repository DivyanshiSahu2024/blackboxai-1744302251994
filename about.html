About This Web3 DApp

This Web3 DApp is designed to provide users with a seamless experience in interacting with the blockchain. Users can connect their wallets, view their balances, and send transactions with ease.

Built using modern web technologies, this application leverages the power of decentralized finance (DeFi) and blockchain technology to empower users in managing their digital assets.

Key Features:

  • Connect your MetaMask wallet
  • View your ETH balance
  • Send transactions to any Ethereum address
  • Monitor network status and connection

We hope you enjoy using this DApp and explore the world of decentralized applications!
